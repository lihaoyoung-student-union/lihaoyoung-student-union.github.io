Come and Visit Art of Stone Carving

Thurs, April 24, 1:30-4pm
The Beijing Stone Carving Art Museum is a thematic museum dedicated to the collection, research, and exhibition of stone carvings from the Beijing area. The museum houses thousands of stone artifacts, spanning from the Eastern Han Dynasty to the Ming and Qing Dynasties. The collection is diverse, including steles, epitaphs, statues, sutra pillars, and stone sculptures.
